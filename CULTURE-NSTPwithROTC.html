Activity Name:

ALL NSTP EVENT WITH ROTC


Category:

Culture


Organizer:

SILLIMAN UNIVERSITY


Date of Event:

12/02/2023

WHAT?

The All NSTP Event with ROTC took place at 3 pm, coinciding with a thunderstorm. This event marked the culmination of activities for ROTC cadets in the first semester. Despite challenging weather conditions, the cadets showcased their dedication by marching through the rain, providing a captivating performance for the audience. As a spectator, it was inspiring to witness the commitment of the cadets to their activity even in adverse weather conditions.

SO WHAT?

The All NSTP Event with ROTC offered a glimpse into the unwavering dedication of ROTC cadets, particularly evident in their persistence during a thunderstorm. The resilience displayed by the cadets became a source of inspiration, highlighting their commitment to their training. While there may be a tinge of jealousy for not being part of ROTC, the realization of personal limitations makes being an appreciative audience member a valuable role.


NOW WHAT?

The event has left a lasting impression, emphasizing the importance of dedication and commitment to a chosen activity, even in adverse conditions. The experience has inspired a renewed appreciation for the discipline instilled by ROTC training. Moving forward, I am motivated to apply the spirit of dedication and perseverance witnessed in the ROTC cadets to my own pursuits, recognizing the value of commitment in achieving goals.



Because of the All NSTP Event with ROTC event, I am inspired to embrace dedication and commitment in my pursuits, drawing motivation from the resilience demonstrated by ROTC cadets during challenging circumstances. The experience has instilled a renewed appreciation for the discipline instilled by ROTC training. Moving forward, I am committed to applying the principles of dedication and perseverance witnessed in the ROTC cadets to my own endeavors, recognizing the transformative power of commitment.
