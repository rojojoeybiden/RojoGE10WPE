groupsSU ROTC CORPS OF SPONSORS

11/23/2023

WHAT?

"Sponsor's Corner," an initiative by the SU ROTC Corps of Sponsors, aimed at supporting the Dumaguete City Jail and contributing to the Casa Esperanza of Angels Foundation. The primary method of contribution involved purchasing food items available at the corner. During my participation, I chose to contribute by buying iced tea. The event served as a reminder of the importance of extending help to those in need.


SO WHAT?

Participating in the Sponsor's Corner event was not only an opportunity to contribute to meaningful causes but also a reminder of the broader responsibility to help those in need. The initiative by the SU ROTC Corps of Sponsors showcased the power of collective action in making a positive impact on the community. The act of purchasing items to support charitable causes, such as the Dumaguete City Jail and Casa Esperanza of Angels Foundation, underscored the significance of small individual contributions in creating a larger, positive change.


NOW WHAT?

The Sponsor's Corner event has left a lasting impression, reinforcing the idea that even small individual contributions can collectively make a significant impact on charitable causes. This experience serves as a catalyst for a continued commitment to helping those in need. Moving forward, I am motivated to actively seek opportunities to contribute to similar initiatives, recognizing the importance of community support in addressing social issues.



Because of the Sponsor's Corner event, I am inspired to actively contribute to charitable causes and support initiatives that aim to help those in need. The experience has reinforced the notion that small individual actions, when combined, can lead to meaningful and positive changes in the community. Moving forward, I am committed to actively seeking and participating in opportunities that contribute to the well-being of others and make a positive impact on society.
