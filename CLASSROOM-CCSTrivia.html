Activity Name:

CCS TRIVIA


Category:

Classroom


Organizer:

CONFEDERATES STUDENT COUNCIL


Date of Event:

08/24/2023

WHAT?

The CCS Trivia, an engaging event created by the Confederate Student Council of CCS, brought students together for an intellectually stimulating experience. The trivia encompassed a wide range of topics related to CCS, spanning from coding questions to the entertaining challenge of identifying faculty members through blurry photos. The event aimed to test participants' knowledge about all things CCS, fostering a sense of camaraderie and friendly competition.


SO WHAT?

Participating in the CCS Trivia proved to be a delightful and enjoyable experience. The diversity of questions, ranging from technical coding queries to the light-hearted identification of faculty members, added a dynamic and entertaining element to the event. Although I didn't secure any prizes, the fun and camaraderie among participants made the event memorable. The trivia not only tested my knowledge but also provided an opportunity to appreciate the breadth of topics within CCS in a lighthearted setting.


NOW WHAT?

The CCS Trivia event has sparked an interest in exploring diverse aspects of CCS beyond the standard academic curriculum. The fun and engaging format of the trivia has inspired me to delve deeper into coding and other facets of CCS, fostering a holistic understanding of the field. Moving forward, I am inclined to participate in similar events to broaden my knowledge and connect with fellow students who share a passion for the diverse facets of computer studies.



Because of this event, I am motivated to explore various aspects of CCS beyond my academic coursework, recognizing the richness and diversity within the field. The CCS Trivia, despite not winning prizes, has instilled a sense of curiosity and camaraderie, encouraging me to actively engage in future events that promote both knowledge and enjoyment within the CCS community.
