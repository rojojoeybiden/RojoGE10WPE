Activity Name:

AESTHETICS COMMITTEE PREPARATION FOR XMAS PARTY


Category:

Community


Organizer:

CCS AESTHETICS COMMITTEE


Date of Event:

12/05-15/2023

WHAT?

As a member of the Aesthetics Committee, I played a significant role in the preparation for the CCS Christmas Party. Contributing to various aspects, including designing signs for the photobooth and freedom wall, as well as participating in creating paper snowmen and snowflakes, I embraced a central role in shaping the visual elements of the event. While the experience became one of the highlights of my college journey, it coincided with challenging timing, diverting attention from more pressing assignments.


SO WHAT?

Despite the challenges, being part of the Aesthetics Committee for the Christmas Party was a fulfilling and enjoyable experience. The camaraderie and sense of shared purpose with fellow committee members showcased the power of friendship in creating positive and memorable moments. Although the timing posed difficulties with other responsibilities, the joy derived from contributing to the party's preparation outweighed the challenges, highlighting the significance of teamwork and shared experiences.


NOW WHAT?

The experience with the Aesthetics Committee has emphasized the value of camaraderie and shared efforts in creating meaningful experiences. While acknowledging the need for better time management, I don't regret the time spent on the Christmas Party preparation. Moving forward, I am inspired to continue fostering connections and actively participating in collaborative efforts that contribute to a positive and vibrant college community.



Because of my involvement in the Aesthetics Committee preparation for the Christmas Party, I am inspired to prioritize camaraderie and shared experiences in my college journey. The power of friendship, evident in the joyous moments created during the preparation, serves as a reminder of the importance of community and teamwork. Moving forward, I am committed to contributing to the vibrant college experience by actively participating in collaborative efforts that enhance the sense of community.
