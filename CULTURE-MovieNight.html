Activity Name:

MOVIE NIGHT


Category:

Culture


Organizer:

CONFEDERATES STUDENT COUNCIL


Date of Event:

08/25/2023

WHAT?

Movie Night, a straightforward event as the name suggests, offered students the opportunity to enjoy a cinematic experience for a nominal fee of Php20. The Confederate Student Council (CSC) organized this event, spanning three days, where students could gather to watch movies. Despite attending only on Day 2, the featured film was "Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse." The screening took place at ATA Hall, showcasing the versatility of the venue for various events.


SO WHAT?

Participating in Movie Night provided a simple yet enjoyable experience. The event offered a relaxed setting for students to unwind and enjoy a popular film at an affordable price. Watching "Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" added an extra layer of excitement, making it a memorable evening. The utilization of ATA Hall for such events demonstrated the university's commitment to providing diverse recreational opportunities for students.


NOW WHAT?

The experience at Movie Night highlighted the importance of affordable and accessible recreational activities for students. The event served as a reminder of the value of communal gatherings for shared enjoyment. Moving forward, I am inclined to participate in similar events, recognizing the significance of such opportunities for relaxation and community building within the university setting.



Because of this event, I am reminded of the importance of affordable and accessible recreational activities for students. Movie Night has inspired me to actively seek out and engage in similar events that contribute to the overall well-being and enjoyment of the university community. The utilization of ATA Hall for such gatherings showcases the potential for diverse and inclusive events that bring students together for shared experiences.
