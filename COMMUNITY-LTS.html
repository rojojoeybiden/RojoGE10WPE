Activity Name:

LTS CHRISTMAS OUTREACH


Category:

Community


Organizer:

NSTP-LTS


Date of Event:

12/02/2023

WHAT?

The LTS Christmas Outreach, organized by NSTP-LTS, involved students from the LTS program going to Barangay Candau-ay for a festive outreach event. The primary objective was to bring joy to less fortunate children by providing them with gifts and engaging in entertaining games. In addition to the holiday spirit, the outreach aimed to share knowledge by imparting a story about Adam and Eve to the children.


SO WHAT?

Participating in the LTS Christmas Outreach revealed the disparity in living conditions between us and the children in Barangay Candau-ay. The outreach became an opportunity to spread the Christmas spirit and create a memorable experience for the less fortunate children. Teaching them about Adam and Eve added an educational dimension to the event, fostering a sense of connection and sharing during the holiday season.


NOW WHAT?

The LTS Christmas Outreach has left a meaningful impression, highlighting the importance of giving back and sharing joy during festive seasons. The experience has inspired a continued commitment to engaging in outreach activities that contribute positively to the community. Moving forward, I am motivated to actively participate in initiatives that bring joy and knowledge to those who may be less fortunate.



Because of the LTS Christmas Outreach, I am inspired to continue participating in outreach activities that bring joy and knowledge to communities in need. The experience has deepened my understanding of the importance of giving back, especially during festive seasons. Moving forward, I am committed to actively contributing to the well-being of others, fostering a sense of community and connection.
