WHAT?

The Intramurals Men's Volleyball Tryouts took place at Ravello Ballfield, serving as the platform for boys to showcase their skills and determine their eligibility to represent CCS during the upcoming Intramurals in February 2024. While the primary purpose was to assess athletic abilities, the event also provided an opportunity for camaraderie and team building.

NOW WHAT?

The Intramurals Men's Volleyball Tryouts have inspired a shift in perspective, encouraging a more active and participatory approach to sports. Moving forward, I am determined to step out of my comfort zone, shed unnecessary fears, and actively engage in sports and other communal activities.
