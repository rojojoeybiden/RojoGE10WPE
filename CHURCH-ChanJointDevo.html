churchNARRA COTTAGE & CHANNON HALL - JOINT DEVOTION

groups SUSG DORM LIFE COMMITTEE

10/12/2023

WHAT?

The Narra Cottage and Channon Hall Joint Devotion brought together dormers from two distinct residences for a shared church event. The gathering served as a moment of relaxation for the dormers, offering prayers and good wishes as they approached the upcoming midterm exams. Beyond the spiritual aspect, the event fostered a sense of community and interaction between the residents of Narra Cottage and Channon Hall.


SO WHAT?

The joint devotion, while centered around a church event, extended beyond mere religious observance. It functioned as a collective effort to provide comfort and well-wishes to dormers ahead of midterm exams. The inclusion of snacks and games elevated the experience, transforming it into a wholesome and enjoyable gathering. The interaction between residents of Narra Cottage and Channon Hall added a layer of community building, showcasing the value of shared moments beyond the academic context.


NOW WHAT?

The Joint Devotion has left a positive impact, highlighting the importance of community and support within the dormitory environment. The experience has inspired a greater appreciation for shared moments of relaxation and encouragement. Moving forward, I am inclined to actively participate in similar events, recognizing the significance of building connections with fellow dormers and fostering a supportive and inclusive living community.



Because of the Narra Cottage and Channon Hall Joint Devotion, I am inspired to actively engage in community-building events within the dormitory setting. The experience has reinforced the importance of shared moments of relaxation and encouragement, contributing to a positive and inclusive living environment. Moving forward, I am eager to contribute to the sense of community and support among dorm residents, fostering a warm and welcoming atmosphere within Narra Cottage and Channon Hall.
